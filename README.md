This project is a personal portfolio website created with HTML5 and CSS3, designed to serve as a professional online presence and digital resume. The website highlights your skills, projects, and achievements in a clean, responsive, and user‑friendly layout. It is lightweight, fast, and requires no external frameworks or build tools, making it easy to maintain and customize.
The portfolio includes dedicated sections such as About Me, Projects, Skills, and Contact, allowing visitors to quickly learn about your background and explore your work. The design emphasizes clarity and accessibility, ensuring that the site looks great across devices — from desktops to mobile phones.
This project is ideal for developers, designers, or professionals who want to showcase their work online without relying on complex setups. With simple HTML and CSS, you can easily update content, adjust styling, and personalize the site to reflect your unique brand. Deployment is straightforward and can be done for free using platforms like GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel, making it accessible to anyone who wants to share their portfolio with the world.
